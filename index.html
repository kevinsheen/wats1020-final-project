Smokey Brights

Smokey Brights are from Seattle, WA. They are a five piece band that includes two guitars, a bass, drums, and keyboards. They've been performing at a lot of local haunts including the Tractor Tavern, the Crocodile, Connor Byrne, the Sunset Tavern, and Columbia City Theater. Keep an eye out for upcoming shows and record release news!

Nada Surf

Nada Surf originate from New York where they started playing music together in 1992, that's right, they've been performing together and consistently writing new music for 24 years now! Their most recent album came out in 2016 and is titled You Know Who You Are and was releases on Barsuk Records.

Julien Baker

Julien Baker is a singer/songwriter from Memphis, Tennessee. She recently released a record in 2015 titled Sprained Ankle and toured around the country to help promote it. She played a solo show at the Vera Project in Seattle, WA in February of 2016.
